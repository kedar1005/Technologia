What is Technologia?

Technologia 2026 is an annual National-Level Technical Fest hosted by D. Y. Patil School of Engineering & Management, Kolhapur. The fest is a hub of dynamic competitions, thought-provoking challenges, and opportunities to explore, create, and excel in various domains of technology and innovation.

From entrepreneurial showcases like Shark Tank to hands-on technical tests in Mini Project Competition , the fest is filled with experiences that challenge the mind and fuel creativity. Debate Competition invites participants to battle it out intellectually, while Code Shuffle pushes coders to think fast and code faster.

Gaming enthusiasts can dive into high-intensity action in the BGMI Tournament, and strategists can bid and build in the ever-popular IPL Auction. For those who think outside the box, The Expandables encourages bold ideas and imaginative solutions.
